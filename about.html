Introduction

Hello, my name is Jenni Lintumäki (she, her) and I am a finnish-filipino visual communication designer based in Espoo, Finland. I have a BA education in visual communication design from Aalto University School of Arts, Design and Architecture, and I am currently completing my MA studies. I will graduate by the end of fall semester (December 2021). During my studies, I have focused on typography, layout design, information design, and learned to incorporate coding into the design process.

I design by organizing, categorizing and labeling data, which is how I process information and solve problems -- I enjoy creating timetables, lists and mindmaps. This is also why I enjoy designing using code, although I am still a beginner. Above all, I aim to create clear, easily understandable and inclusive design for everyone.
