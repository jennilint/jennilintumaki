Project Description

Fast fashion has negative societal and environmental impacts, including modern-day slavery, child labor, and pollution. As a fast fashion victim myself, I wanted to design a solution that brings awareness to this topic, since I was not aware of these problems until a couple years ago.

Tieto Pukee (Information Suits) is a tool that provides customizable information about the dark side of fast fashion. This information can then be printed to clothes, for example on a t-shirt, using transfer paper. Thus, anyone can spread information on their own clothes, as if you were a walking poster, and do it in your own personal style. It is better to print informative statements on clothes you would wear in everyday life instead of printing new t-shirts as provoking posters, which was the original idea. During my research, I realized how much blood and sweat goes into making textiles, so we should not treat them the same way as posters and flyers.
