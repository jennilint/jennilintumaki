Project Description

A large poster created for an exhibition in the theme anthropocene in 2019. Poster is a digital collage created by painting with acrylics, scanning trash and combining them with images to create a doomsday landscape. This collage poster was inspired by christian illustrations of Jesus’ second coming and the movie Interstellar. I believe that if the Earth becomes unlivable due to climate change, humans might move to another planet in order to survive. Just like in the Bible, only chosen (wealthy) ones will be able to escape the planet, and the rest will burn in hell (the Earth).
